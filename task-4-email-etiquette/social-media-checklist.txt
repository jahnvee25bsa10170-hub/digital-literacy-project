## Social Media Checklist for College Students

# Do’s
1. Think before you post anything online
2. Keep your account secure and private where possible
3. Share content that is respectful and meaningful
4. Be polite while interacting with others
5. Give credit when using someone else’s work
6. Stay aware of what you are posting and who can see it

# Don’ts
7. Don’t share sensitive personal information online
8. Don’t post when you are angry or emotional
9. Don’t engage in unnecessary arguments or negativity
10. Don’t spread information without checking if it is true
11. Don’t overshare details about your daily life
12. Don’t ignore basic privacy and security settings
