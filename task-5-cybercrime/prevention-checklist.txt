## Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students)
Don’t click on unknown links or messages, even if they look official
Always check the sender’s email or phone number before trusting any message
Keep strong passwords for all your accounts and don’t reuse the same password everywhere
Enable two-factor authentication (OTP/login verification) wherever possible
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts like banking or email

## UPI / Financial Safety
Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone (no real service asks for it)
Don’t approve any “collect request” on UPI apps unless you are 100% sure
Double-check the name before sending money through apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.

## General Awareness
Keep your apps and phone updated to avoid security issues
Log out from devices that are not your own
Be careful while downloading apps—only use trusted sources like Play Store

## Reporting & Help
If you face any cyber fraud, immediately report it on National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
You can also call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for quick assistance
