I made a one page infographic about digital literacy. I used Canva to make it. The infographic talks about what digital literacy means, how to stay safe on the internet, and how to write good emails. I put the infographic in the task-1-presentation folder.

I made accounts on three websites - GitHub, LinkedIn, and HackerRank. On GitHub I wrote a small introduction about myself. On LinkedIn I filled my education details. On HackerRank I made my profile. I took screenshots of all three profiles and put them in the task-2-portfolio folder.
